Husky Experience: Thrive

Thrive weekly messages are designed to help you succeed wherever you are in your University career. These messages address four interconnected dimensions of the Husky Experience: Know Yourself, Know the World, Make Your Way, and Make Sense.

Messages are intentionally scheduled to provide timely advice, but read and explore them anytime - your husky experience is more than a major.

Know Yourself

Know your interests and what engages you. Gain experiences that will test ideas you have about who you are. Build a supportive social network and make decisions in your best interest. Understand how tending to your wellness provides the foundation to success in all other activities.

Know the World

Know how your education relates to the world. Understand the relationship between major and career, and the relationships between different disciplines. Ensure you have the knowledge and skills to achieve academically and contribute to a changing world.

More About Thrive

Created by faculty, staff, and experienced students, Thrive messages offer guidance to help students:

  • Explore opportunities and make hte most of learning experiences
  • Connect to other members of the campus community and to important resources
  • Construct the foundation for a healthy, fulfilling life and career

We plan to add new Thrive messages and resources each academic year.
